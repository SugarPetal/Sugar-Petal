About

At Sugar Petal, we create handmade desserts inspired mostly by Middle Eastern flavors and traditions, blended with a modern, delicate touch. Every treat is baked in small batches using quality ingredients, traditional techniques, and plenty of care—never mass-produced.

We proudly offer local delivery only, allowing us to focus on freshness and detail from our kitchen to your door. Each item is made to order and crafted with heart.
